Universal bisquit

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Flour 100 g
Sugar 150 g

Step 1: Mix sugar, flour and egg yolks together.

Step 2: Whip egg whites with a mixer until stiff peaks.

Step 3: Gently mix sugar, flour and yolks with egg whites. Put everything into a baking form.

Step 4: Bake bisquit at 180 degrees C 40-45 minutes.

Spaghetti bolognese

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1 pack
Ground meat 400 g
Tomato paste 70 g
Tomatoes 4 pcs
Oregano by taste
Basil by taste
Vegetable oil for roasting

Step 1: Roast ground meat for 15 minutes. Add salt and pepper. Boil water for spaghetti.

Step 2: Add tomatoes with tomato paste to meat. Simmer bolognese sauce for 10 minutes. Boil spaghetti for 8 minutes.

Step 3: Add oregano, basil. Simmer for more 3-5 minutes. Get the spaghetti out.

Step 4: Serve the dishes.

Buon appetito!
